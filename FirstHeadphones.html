First Headphones

The first headphones were invented in 1910 by Nathaniel Baldwin. He created them for the purpose of better heairing the sermons at his church. After working diligently to invent them, Baldwin's prototype was bought by the U.S Navy. His design became the basis for all headphones that came after it.
