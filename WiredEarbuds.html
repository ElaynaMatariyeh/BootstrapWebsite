Wired Earbuds

Wired headphones are a valuable part of headphone history. They are extremely discreet while also being hard to lose. They are more compactthan the wired headset which allows for easy transportation. Because of their headphone jack, they are compaible with an phone or computer, adding to their versatility. Their lack of charging also makes them better than modern headphones at times.
