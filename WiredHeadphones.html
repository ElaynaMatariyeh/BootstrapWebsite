Wired Headphones

The first wired headphones were a major upgrade from the first headphones. These headhones contained a headphone jack, making them more compact than previous models. Because they could be transpoted easily, people could plug their headphones into any computer for easy access. These headphones made it possible for future innovations, such as the wireless headphone.
